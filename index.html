MST @ West Humber is a pre-AP program with an enriched focus on Mathematics, Science and Technology.

MATH

Mathematics is designed to challenge and inspire students in the MST Program at West Humber Collegiate Institute. It offers a comprehensive curriculum that goes above and beyond the standard curriculum. Students in Grades 9 and 10 learn and tackle many math concepts that will help them in their future studies. Students in Grades 11 and 12 take special math courses as well as AP courses such as AP Calculus. This math course encourages critical thinking and problem-solving, preparing students for success.


SCIENCE


Science in the MST Program is designed to ensure students have a thorough understanding of a variety of topics. Students in grades 9 to 10 are introduced to various scientific principles. By Grades 11 and 12, science will be divided into different branches, allowing students to pursue the course of their choice (Biology, Chemistry, etc.). In addition, the MST program offers AP science courses in Grade 12.


TECH


Technology is one of the program's shining Jewels. The MST program teaches students about the current technological world as well as how to code and be good digital citizens. Beginning in Grade 10, MST students will study computer science, by coding in Python. MST students begin programming classes a year earlier than usual, which can help to develop and foster an interest in the field.
